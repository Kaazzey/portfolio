Plog de Dag

Plog de Dag is a concept, where you pick up trash while jogging to create a cleaner Amsterdam together. To make this attracting to everyone I've made a mobile and desktop onepager, changing the design of I Amsterdam a bit to make Plog de Dag feel like it has its own identity.

  • Year: 2020
  • Client: HvA & I Amsterdam
  • Role: UX Designer & Visual Designer
