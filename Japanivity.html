Japanivity

Japanivity is a concept for an app and website that travelers could use to plan their trips and activities in Japan. This project's main focus was C-strategies and the phases of the trip wherein the user would use the app or website. You could plan your entire trip at home on the website, then modify your trip on your destination with the app.
