De Bibliotheek Booktracker

Books are easily tracked in highschool libraries with De Bibliotheek (translation: The Library) booktracker app. Here children between the ages of 12 to 15 can look for a book by entering their reading difficulty, genre and age. After entering these criteria they'll be presented with a list of books matching that criteria. Here they can see if the book they're looking for is available, and if so, on what shelf they can find their book. They can add the books to a wishlist, which they'll be able to e-mail to themselves or print out right there. Then they can take their receipt to hire the book they want to read.
