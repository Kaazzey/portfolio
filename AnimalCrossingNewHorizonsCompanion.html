Animal Crossing: New Horizons Companion

Animal Crossing: New Horizons Companion is a concept for a an app that helps you manage and/or complete your daily tasks from the game within the app itself. This is particularly handy since some of the tasks in the game are time-based, and unable to be completed after those set times. This can be frustrating for people who aren't able to play withtin the set times, who'd still want to complete those tasks. With this app you won't have to drag your Nintendo Switch to awkward places, and you'll be able to complete time based tasks during your lunch break at work for example.
