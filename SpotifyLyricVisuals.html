Spotify Lyric Visuals

The current Spotify lyrics are pretty static, thus I've made a redesign of the current lyric section of the Spotify app, making it more fun and engaging for the listener. The lyrics are supposedly animated and will come into view with a little animation regarding the theme of the song on the right timing.
