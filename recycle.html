Recycle Rainbow

This is an interface made out of 30 self made photo's linked to the Dutch Design Week subject: The Greater Number. In this interface you'll see everyday products, where the manufacturer has given the consumer the option to recycle the packaging. Since the colours of the packaging is colourful, I've decided to create a little Recycle Rainbow.
